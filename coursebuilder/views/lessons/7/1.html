Incorporating feedback is necessary for designing an app. Especially in the app’s early stage, incorporating feedback increases the quality and usability of the app because the more people test the app's user-interface, the more feedback is available to maximize the app's usability.
This lesson is great for professional development. Beyond Technovation Challenge, a goal of this lesson is to be able to incorporate feedback and criticism—which can help in school, college, and career. Being able to handle constructive criticism and remove an emotional reaction to other’s feedback can contribute to self-improvement.
